GRID SURVIVAL // QUICK INSTALL BRIEF
===================================

Welcome to Grid Survival, a pulse-pounding arena where the floor literally vanishes beneath your feet. Gear up, grab a friend, and prove you can ride out the collapse.

MENU FLOW
---------
- ENTER / LEFT CLICK: advance through title, mode, and character select screens.
- ESC / BACK BUTTON: return to the previous screen at any time.
- NUMBER KEYS (1-3): instantly choose Solo vs AI, Local Multiplayer, or Online placeholders on the mode screen.
- CHARACTER SELECT: hover or use arrow/WASD keys to preview heroes, ENTER/SPACE to lock in, BACKSPACE to undo.

IN-GAME CONTROLS
----------------
SOLO OR PLAYER ONE
- Move: W / A / S / D
- Jump: SPACE
- Power Ability: Q (when Power Orbs are charged)
- Pause / Exit Run: ESC

PLAYER TWO (LOCAL MULTIPLAYER)
- Move: Arrow Keys
- Jump: Right SHIFT
- Power Ability: /
- Pause / Exit Run: ESC (either player)

GAMEPLAY ESSENTIALS
-------------------
- Survive on the shrinking grid by reading tile warnings and keeping your footing.
- Collect Power Orbs to unlock your character's signature ability; unleash it with the Power key for clutch saves.
- Shields block a single hazard hit; watch the edge glow to know when it is about to expire.
- Click the speaker icon in the HUD to toggle music mid-run.

WHY PLAY NOW
------------
- Hand-painted arenas, animated water, and cinematic shield effects keep every fall spectacular.
- Four distinct heroes (Caveman, Giant Goblin, Ninja, Viking Leader) with bespoke animations and powers.
- Drop-in local co-op lets you challenge a friend instantly with no setup, just press START and fight the collapse.
